Company

  • Octahedron

    Octahedron is an IT company founded by young entrepreneurs in Campina Grande.

    We are a company specialized in developing web solutions for environments running on cloud computing, with a focus on social, distributed and high availability applications. We seek to develop solutions that meet adequately the needs of customers, always providing a good user experience.

    Octahedron is located in Campina Grande, Paraíba, Brazil. Campina Grande is the most important city in the interior of Paraíba which has approximately 380,000 inhabitants and hosts the Federal University of Campina Grande. The Computer Science course in UFCG is one of the oldest in the area and celebrated throughout Brazil. In 2008, it obtained grade 5 (maximum) in the criteria evaluated by ENADE/MEC, has earned the best rating among the courses of the north, northeast and midwest. Only five other courses had the same assessment. The city also stands out as a tech city in Brazil.

    It was in this scenario, convinced that cloud computing represents a new paradigm for computing, and decided to participate of this moment, that Danilo Queiroz and Vítor Avelino founded Octahedron - a company specialized in developing applications for the cloud.
