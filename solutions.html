  • Why on the cloud?

    Let's take a look at the main cloud computing advantages? ;)

    Scalability on demand (pay-as-you-go)

    If an organization has periods of time in which their computing resource needs are much higher or lower than normal, cloud technologies can deal with those changes. The organization pays for the IT resources it actually uses; it does not have to maintain multiple sets of artificially high levels of resources to handle peak demands.

    Improving Business Processes

    An organization and its suppliers and partners can share data and applications in the cloud, allowing everyone involved to focus on the business process instead of the infrastructure that hosts it.

    Minimizing Startup Costs

    For companies that are just starting out, organizations in emerging market, cloud computing greatly reduces startup costs. The new organization starts with an infrastructure already in place, so the time and other resources that would be spent on building a data center are borne by the cloud provider.

  • How we work

    Methodology

    We use an agile development and management methodology on our projects. This type of methodology aims to deal better with changing priorities and requirements as to minimize project risks. It also allows to the customer a better monitoring of and involvement in managerial decision-making team.

    Ideally, at the end of each cycle is delivered a working version of the system under development to the customer. This practice allows the customer to provide feedback about their impressions of the system throughout the process.

  • Open source projects

    Cotopaxi

    Cotopaxi is a Java Framework for development of web applications, focused on the cloud. The main objective is make the development of applications more agile, providing access to all the resources needed on the cloud and providing abstractions to the controller layer. Using Cotopaxi, developers keep the focus on the model/business layer and can make applications scalable running on the cloud.
