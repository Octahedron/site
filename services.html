Services

  • Assistance and integration

    The Octahedron's assistance and integration is a service provided near the client seeking to optimize business processes. The aim is to introduce and implement solutions, through the use of cloud computing, that meet customer needs.

    Our team should consider using alternatives to cloud computing that represent optimal solutions, ie to consider the cost and agility, and elaborate a solutions plan, presenting a detailed study of impact of each solution, to finally establish together the client an integration plan.

  • Projetcs on demand

    The development of customized software aims at provide new solutions, or new/customized features for specific customers.

    The main challenge of this kind of activity is understand cutomers' desires in order to develop a solution that brings the agility to him. Another important aspect is that the solution developed needs to introduce a good user experience.

    Thus, we should always work as closely as possible the customer, involving them in management activity and monitoring of the project. On this way, it provides that the solution is enhanced and validated throughout their development, thus increasing the chances of project success.

  • Software as a service

    The availability of a solution/software as a service (SaaS) aims to meet the market demands of smaller clients. The creation of a SaaS reduces the cost of client's application and centralize the operations on just one application, which can have multiple clients, facilitating their management.

    The goal is to provide a single solution, customizable, centralized and with low cost that meets a customer demand with less capacity to invest in IT.
